What Makes Our Coffee Special?


Our beans are sourced from local plantations, where the unique climate and fertile soil contribute to a distinctive flavor profile that you won’t find anywhere else.
We prioritize sustainable farming practices, ensuring that our coffee not only tastes great but also supports the local community. Each cup is carefully crafted by our skilled baristas, who bring out the natural aromas and flavors, allowing you to experience the true essence of Sri Lankan coffee.

Join us to savor the unique taste of our carefully curated brews and discover the story behind every sip!

Coffee with a Conscience

MR:Maya Brooks

We're proud to serve more than just great coffee — we serve purpose. Every cup is made using ethically sourced beans from farmers who share our commitment to sustainability and quality. Our takeaway cups are 100% compostable, and we encourage our community to bring their own reusable mugs with a small discount as a thank-you. From recycling and energy-efficient equipment to locally sourced pastries, every decision we make is guided by our love for both people and the planet.
